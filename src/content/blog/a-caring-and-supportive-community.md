As our orphanage takes shape, we envision it to be more than just a place to live and study. It will be a vibrant community where children can form strong bonds with their peers and caring adults who serve as their mentors and role models.

By offering a secure and stable home, we hope to give these children a sense of belonging and the opportunity to grow and thrive. Comfortable living spaces, nutritious meals, and access to healthcare are the foundation — but the relationships built here are what truly last a lifetime.
