Every child deserves a bright future filled with love, care, and opportunity. We are building an orphanage in the vibrant city of Kaolack, Senegal, to provide shelter, food, and education to the most vulnerable among us — the orphans.

Construction is underway on comfortable living spaces designed to feel like a real home, not an institution. Each dormitory is planned around natural light, shared common rooms, and quiet corners for study and rest.

We're grateful to every donor who has helped us reach this stage, and we invite you to walk this journey with us.
